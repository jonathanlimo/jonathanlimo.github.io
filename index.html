SQL Data Analysis: Grocery Store Sales Optimization

A data analysis project focused on optimizing product stocking and pricing strategies for FoodYum, a grocery store chain. This involved extensive **SQL for data cleaning, transformation, and analysis** on a `products` dataset.

Key responsibilities included:

  • Handling **missing data imputation** for product attributes (e.g., replacing missing `weight` and `price` with **overall medians** using SQL aggregates).
  • Replacing categorical missing values (`product_type`, `brand`, `stock_location`) with 'Unknown'.
  • Analyzing sales trends and product distribution to inform strategic pricing decisions.
  • Ensuring data integrity and preparing datasets for business intelligence reporting.
